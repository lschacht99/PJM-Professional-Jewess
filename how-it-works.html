How it works

From applicant to matched, the real path.

Curated previews, your preferences, a careful human match — never a dating-app feed of people to shop through.

01
You join by application
A curated network, not an open directory.
02
You say who you are
Your field, your stage, your community, your goals.
03
You name a few you’d like to learn from
A preference, not a booking.
04
The team makes the match
Weighed by fit, stage, observance, and her capacity.
05
Now it’s you and her
Her full profile opens. No one else is shopping for her.
06
You schedule the first call
Her real availability — no WhatsApp tag.
07
You grow in your cohort
A small feed of women a few steps ahead of you.
08
One day, you mentor someone
The reason any of this exists.
What we match on

Both sides fill these in at signup.

★
Field & specialty
A litigator for a future lawyer, not just “someone in law.”
★
Stage & goals
Seminary, college, first job, or a second act.
★
Community & observance
A mentor who understands the life, not only the career. Always optional.
★
Availability & capacity
One-time call, ongoing, or a group — matched so no one is overloaded.
